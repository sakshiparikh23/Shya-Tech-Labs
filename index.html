#COVID-19 Be INFORMED, Be PREPARED, Be SMART, Be SAFE

At Shyalabs, it is not just our team of dedicated people but also everyone who seeks our service that join hands to build a stronger network, one that vows to shield us in times of need, and one that grows stronger in the face of the not-so-bright days.

While preparation, production and propagation of everything beginning from the tiniest health equipments to surgical instruments, X-ray machines, scanners, drug production chains, medication right up to medical advice is currently dependent on technology, we offer an unique solution to provide as many of these solutions possible from a single, cumulative platform.

Our goal is to not only connect the ones in search of help to the ones who can provide it to them but at the same time to ensure that we create a better world by stepping up and coming together to help each other. This is possible only when the right technology meets the right people.

The situational helplessness that humanity has faced upon being exposed to the COVID-19 pandemic has pointed out the immediate necessities to facilitate civilisation, and hence life itself on and within the heart of the planet. One of the first methods that can be opted for ensuring survival is boosting the healthcare system. Given the large number of seekers of life, and given the comparatively smaller number of experts who can provide them health service – technology could be the only mediator that would help bridge such an inevitable gap.
